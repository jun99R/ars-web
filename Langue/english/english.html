Agence Rebi Services S.A.R.L

is a multidisciplinary service provider, proudly operating under Congolese law within the OHADA system. Our commitment lies in delivering high-quality goods and services while ensuring our equipment is modern and reliable.

Services We Offer

we offer a diverse range of services designed to meet the varied needs of our clients. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to specific requirements. Below is an overview of our key services:

  • Civil Engineering and Construction

    We undertake all civil engineering and construction activities, offering unbeatable prices for our high-quality services.

  • Maintenance

    We offer corrective, predictive, and preventive maintenance, along with industrial maintenance to ensure efficient operations and minimal downtime.

  • Cleaning and Disinfection

    We manage sanitation through cleaning workspaces, offices, and handling waste from treatments, all in line with ISO 9001 safety and environmental standards.

  • Mining-related goods

    We have the necessary machinery and equipment for efficient and effective mining operations, as well as essential reagents to support the process.

  • Pharmaceutical Products, Consumables, and Medical Equipment

    We supply essential pharmaceutical products, medical consumables, and equipment, ensuring high standards for effective healthcare and treatment.

  • Agricultural Products and Inputs

    We provide a range of agricultural products and inputs, including seeds, fertilizers, and soil conditioners, to support efficient and productive farming.
